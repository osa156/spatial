About Us

Leading the Future of Healthcare

Founded in 1999, MediCare Plus Hospital has grown from a small community clinic to one of the nation's premier healthcare institutions. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and compassionate care has made us a trusted name in healthcare.

With over 1,000 beds, 500+ physicians, and 50 specialized departments, we provide comprehensive care that addresses every aspect of our patients' health. Our state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology ensure that our patients receive the best possible treatment.

Accredited by JCI
Magnet Recognized

Our Journey

Hospital History

1999

Foundation

MediCare Plus was established as a small community hospital with 50 beds and a mission to provide accessible healthcare.

2005

Expansion

Added specialized cardiac care center and increased capacity to 200 beds to serve growing community needs.

2012

Research Center

Opened state-of-the-art research facility focusing on cancer treatment and cardiovascular research.

2018

National Recognition

Ranked among top 10 hospitals in the nation by U.S. News & World Report for the first time.

2024

Digital Transformation

Launched comprehensive telemedicine platform and AI-powered diagnostic tools.
